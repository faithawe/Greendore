Typography system

DM Serif Display

Headlines, plant names, emotionally charged moments. Weight 400 regular + italic.

DM Sans

Body copy, labels, data. Weights 300 (body), 400 (normal), 500 (emphasis).

Transition rules

Horizontal slide, 320ms, ease-out cubic

Background cross-fade 400ms underlying slide

Shared element: pod frame morphs into home hero card

Color usage rules

Soil night — only on immersive/dark screens
Canopy — primary buttons, payoff screen bg
New leaf — labels, active states, focus rings
Terracotta — step indicators, accents only
Clay — italic display text on dark canopy bg only
Linen — all light-mode screen backgrounds
