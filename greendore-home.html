Content rules — the botanist voice

What every dynamic string must and must not say

Greeting lineTime-of-day aware: "Good morning" / "Good afternoon" / "Good evening" — never just "Hello"
Insight cardAlways uses plant name. Always contextualises the current stage. Always ends positively or forward-looking. Max 2 sentences. Written in first person from plant's perspective OR botanist speaking to user.
Task descriptionsInclude plant name + specific data + human timeframe. "Vesper's tank is at 34%. She'll need water within 18 hours." Not "Water level low."
Sensor status labelsIdeal / Optimal / On schedule / Normal / Refill soon / Too dry / Check needed — never raw boolean states like "OK" / "ERROR"
Growth noteAlways explain WHY the current stage looks the way it does. Basil in true-leaf stage looks slow above soil — roots are doubling below. Normalise what beginners fear.
Empty statesIf no tasks today: "Everything is in order today. Just let Vesper grow." Not "No tasks."
