Common questions

Why isn't my plant growing?

Slow growth above soil is almost always root development happening below. If it's been fewer than 5 days since sprouting, this is completely normal.

My pod won't connect

Move your phone within 2m of the pod, ensure Bluetooth is on, and check the pod has power — the front LED should pulse green when ready.

How long until my first harvest?

It depends on what you're growing. Basil takes 21–28 days, mint 18–24, microgreens as little as 10. Your home screen shows the exact countdown for Vesper.

Danger actions — log out & delete

Color, copy, and confirmation behaviour

Log out icon bg#FDEAEA danger-light — warm red tint, not harsh
Log out icon color#D94F4F — used only here and delete account
Log out labelDM Sans 14px 400, #D94F4F. No sub-label needed.
Log out tapOpens a confirmation bottom sheet: "Log out of Greendore? Your pod will keep running." Two buttons: "Log out" (red) + "Cancel" (dismiss).
Delete account labelDM Sans 14px 400, #D94F4F. Sub-label: "Permanently removes all data. Cannot be undone."
Delete account tapTwo-step: first sheet explains what is deleted. "Continue to delete" goes to second sheet asking user to type "DELETE" to confirm. Prevents accidental deletion.
Design ruleNever use bright red backgrounds. Danger is communicated through text and icon color only, on a pale background. Calm the user, don't alarm them.

Navigation rules — settings screens

How all sub-screens behave

Sub-screen entryAll settings sub-screens use push-right 300ms ease-out. Same animation as the main settings push from Garden.
Back from sub-screenBack arrow top-left. Returns to main Settings screen with push-left. Main settings scroll position is preserved.
Save actions"Save" button appears top-right only when there is unsaved state (Edit Profile, Pod Settings). It is absent when the screen is read-only or auto-saves.
Auto-saveToggles and sliders save immediately on change — no Save button needed. A small toast "Saved" appears bottom-center for 1.6s to confirm.
Bottom navVisible on all settings screens. Tapping any tab dismisses the entire settings stack and navigates to that tab. Settings push is not remembered — re-entering settings always starts from the main settings screen.
Deepest levelSettings has maximum 2 levels: Main Settings → Sub-screen. There are no third-level screens. Everything resolves within a sub-screen or a bottom sheet.
